Lasagna

Recreate worlds best Bolognese

This Bolognese recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingridients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Red wine
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika spice

Steps:

  1. Start by chopping up the onion
  2. Then move on to heat the butter
  3. Put the onion in the heated butter in the pan
  4. Fry the beef together with the onion
  5. Add your spices to the ground beef
  6. Add red wine together with your spices
  7. Mix the lasagna plates together with the ground beef and put in the oven for 300c for 40 min
